Three steps to the same moment

STEP 1

Everyone installs it

Each listener adds Mowj Rooms to Chrome. It stays dormant everywhere except youtube.com and music.youtube.com.

STEP 2

One person hosts

Open YouTube Music, press play, click the wave bubble, and start a room. Share the six-letter code or the invite link.

STEP 3

Friends join in

They enter the code and land exactly where the song is. Playlists carry over, tracks advance together, and drift is corrected before you can hear it.

Nothing leaves your browser but the beat

Mowj never records, streams, or uploads audio. Each browser plays YouTube Music by itself; the room shares only a track ID, a position, and a timestamp — a few bytes, not the music.

Rooms are anonymous by design: no account, no email, no history. When the host leaves, the room is deleted. Read the privacy policy.
